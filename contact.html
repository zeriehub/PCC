Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking 6-12 months in advance for weddings and 3-6 months for other events, especially for peak season dates.

Do you offer catering services?

Yes, we have in-house catering with customizable menus. We also work with select preferred vendors if you have specific requirements.

Can I schedule a tour?

Absolutely! Contact us to schedule a private tour of our venue. Tours are available by appointment during business hours.

What is included in the rental?

Rental includes the venue space, tables, chairs, basic linens, audio/visual equipment, and parking. Additional services can be added.

Is there a deposit required?

Yes, a deposit is required to secure your date. The amount varies based on the event type and date. Details will be provided in your quote.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellation policies vary by event type and booking date. Full details are provided in your contract. Please contact us for specific information.
